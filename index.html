Millions of people play video games.

What does it take to be the best?

Determination and ability.

APM: Actions per minute. This refers to how many times you "press a button" per minute in a game. Most esports players have an APM of 400 or higher (6.6 actions per second).

Knowledge: Players from each game have to learn game-specific information in order to gain an advantage.
